Our Story

The Daily Grind was founded in 2020 by local artisan baker, Sarah Chen, with a simple mission: to elevate the everyday coffee experience. We believe that quality starts at the source. That's why we meticulously source fair-trade, single-origin beans and use only heritage grain flours for our baked goods.
